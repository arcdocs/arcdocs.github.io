Data Management#

This guide asks a range of questions to consider when planning your data management. Each question has resources for more information.

Checklist

  • Keep the raw data untouched, backed up, and read-only.

  • Create the plan in advance, stick to it, and document everything.

  • Ensure personal data is handled correctly.
